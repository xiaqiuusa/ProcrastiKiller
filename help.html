Getting Started

Welcome to ProcrastiKiller! This guide will help you understand the basics and start your productivity journey.

First Steps

  1. Create your first task in the Tasks tab
  2. Complete tasks to earn coins and XP
  3. Use coins to pull weapons from the gacha
  4. Battle monsters to earn more rewards
  5. Merge weapons to increase their power

üí° Pro Tip

Take your time with tasks! The longer you spend on a task (up to 60 minutes), the more coins and XP you'll earn.

Task Management

Learn how to create, manage, and complete tasks effectively.

Creating Tasks

To create a new task:

  • Go to the Tasks tab
  • Enter your task description in the input field
  • Click "Add Task" to create the task

Completing Tasks

When you're ready to complete a task:

  • Click the "Complete" button next to your task
  • The system will calculate your rewards based on time spent
  • You'll see your earned coins and XP displayed

Gacha System

Learn about the weapon gacha system and how to build your arsenal.

Pulling Weapons

  • Each weapon pull costs 100 coins
  • Weapons have different rarities: Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary
  • Higher rarity weapons have more damage

Weapon Rarities

Common: Wooden Sword (10 damage), Iron Sword (20 damage)
Rare: Steel Sword (35 damage)
Epic: Magic Staff (50 damage)
Legendary: Dragon Blade (100 damage)

Drop Rates

  • Common: 80% chance
  • Rare: 15% chance
  • Epic: 4% chance
  • Legendary: 1% chance

üí° Strategy Tip

Save up your coins and pull multiple weapons at once. You might get lucky with a rare or epic weapon!

Battle System

Master the art of monster hunting and earn rewards.

Monster Spawning

Monsters automatically spawn in the battle arena. Each monster has different stats and rewards.

Monster Types

Goblin: 50 HP, 10 damage, 5 XP cost, 20 coin reward
Orc: 100 HP, 20 damage, 10 XP cost, 40 coin reward
Troll: 200 HP, 35 damage, 20 XP cost, 80 coin reward
Dragon: 500 HP, 80 damage, 50 XP cost, 200 coin reward
Demon Lord: 1000 HP, 150 damage, 100 XP cost, 500 coin reward

Attacking Monsters

  • Select a weapon from the dropdown menu
  • Click "Attack" to deal damage
  • Each attack costs XP based on the monster
  • Defeat the monster to earn coins

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Tip

Use your strongest weapons against tougher monsters. The damage you deal is equal to your weapon's damage stat.

Crafting System

Learn how to merge weapons and increase their power.

Weapon Merging

How to Merge Weapons

  1. Go to the Crafting tab
  2. Click on Slot 1 and select a weapon
  3. Click on Slot 2 and select the same weapon type
  4. Click "Merge Weapons" to combine them
  5. The new weapon will have increased damage

Damage Increase

When you merge two weapons:

  • The damage increases by 0.5% of the original weapon's damage
  • Minimum increase is 1 damage point
  • You can merge the same weapon type multiple times

‚ö†Ô∏è Important

You can only merge weapons of the same type. For example, you can merge two Wooden Swords, but not a Wooden Sword with an Iron Sword.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and their solutions.

Progress Not Saving

Problem: Your progress disappears when you refresh the page.

Solution: Make sure you're not using incognito/private browsing mode, and check that localStorage is enabled in your browser.

Can't Complete Tasks

Problem: The complete button doesn't work.

Solution: Try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache and try again.

Weapons Not Appearing

Problem: Pulled weapons don't show up in inventory.

Solution: Check if you have enough coins (100 per pull). If you do, try refreshing the page.

Monsters Not Spawning

Problem: No monsters appear in the battle arena.

Solution: Monsters spawn automatically. If none appear, wait a few minutes or refresh the page.

If you continue experiencing issues, please contact our support team with details about your browser and the specific problem you're encountering.

Advanced Tips & Strategies

Master the game with these advanced strategies.

Optimal Task Timing

For maximum efficiency:

Weapon Strategy

Smart weapon management:

  • Keep multiple copies of the same weapon for merging
  • Use weaker weapons on easy monsters to save XP
  • Save your strongest weapons for tough battles

Coin Management

Efficient coin usage:

  • Save coins for weapon pulls rather than spending immediately
  • Focus on defeating monsters for steady coin income
  • Balance between pulling new weapons and saving for emergencies
