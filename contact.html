Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?
You can reset your progress by clearing your browser's localStorage. Go to your browser settings, find the storage section, and clear data for our website.
Why aren't I earning coins for completing tasks quickly?
Our system rewards time investment. Tasks completed within 5 minutes earn no coins, but the longer you take (up to 60 minutes), the more coins you earn. This encourages thoughtful task completion.
How do I get better weapons?
Weapons are obtained through the gacha system using coins. Save up 100 coins and try your luck! You can also merge duplicate weapons to increase their damage.
What happens when I defeat a monster?
When you defeat a monster, you earn coins based on the monster's difficulty. The monster will respawn after a short delay, giving you another chance to earn rewards.
Is my progress saved?
Yes! Your progress is automatically saved to your browser's localStorage. As long as you use the same browser and don't clear your data, your progress will be preserved.
Can I play on multiple devices?
Currently, progress is stored locally on each device. We're working on cloud sync functionality for future updates.
